Return & Exchange Policy

Our Commitment to Your Satisfaction

We want you to love your purchase. If you’re not satisfied, you can return or exchange eligible items within 7 days of delivery, subject to the conditions below.

Eligibility for Returns & Exchanges

  • Items must be returned within 7 days from the date of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, in their original condition, and with all tags and packaging intact.
  • Defective or incorrect items are eligible for free return shipping.
  • Non-defective returns/exchanges require the customer to cover return shipping costs.

How to Return or Exchange an Item

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp (+2348142259939) or email (dmoretech44@gmail.com) with your order ID and reason for return/exchange.
  2. We’ll provide a return address and instructions.
  3. Pack the item securely and ship it back to us using a reliable courier.
  4. Once we receive and inspect the item, we’ll process your refund or exchange within 5 business days.

Refunds

Refunds will be issued to your original payment method (e.g., bank account for transfers, card for online payments) or as store credit, based on your preference. Refunds for Pay on Delivery orders will be via bank transfer. Processing may take 3-7 business days after we receive the item.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Personalized or custom-made products.
  • Items marked as “final sale” at purchase.
  • Underwear, swimwear, or other hygienic products, unless defective.

Important Notes

  • We inspect all returned items before issuing refunds or exchanges.
  • Shipping fees for non-defective returns are non-refundable.
  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery to initiate a return/exchange.
  • For defective items, include photos when contacting us for faster processing.

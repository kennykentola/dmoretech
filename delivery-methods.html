Delivery Methods

How We Deliver Your Order

We partner with reliable courier services to ensure your order reaches you safely and on time. Delivery timelines depend on your location, starting from the date of order pickup (confirmation).

Delivery Timelines

  • Ibadan, Oyo State: 3 business days from pickup.
  • Oyo State (outside Ibadan): 5 business days from pickup.
  • Far Locations (outside Oyo State, e.g., Lagos, Abuja): 15 business days from pickup.

Delivery Process

Once your order is confirmed and payment is verified, we dispatch it within 24 hours. You’ll receive a tracking number via WhatsApp (+2348142259939) or email. Contact us to check your delivery status.

Delivery Costs

Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight. Pay on Delivery orders may incur a small handling fee. Contact us for a quote if your location is outside Nigeria.

International Orders

For orders outside Nigeria, delivery may take longer, and customers are responsible for customs duties and taxes. We’ll provide documentation to assist with clearance.

Important Notes

  • Delivery timelines exclude weekends and public holidays.
  • Ensure someone is available to receive the package at the delivery address.
  • Track your order by contacting us on WhatsApp: +2348142259939.
  • Delays may occur due to weather, logistics issues, or customs processing for international orders.
